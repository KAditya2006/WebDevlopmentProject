Treatment Recommendations

Chemical Treatment

Recommended Pesticide: Copper Hydroxide 77% WP
Dosage: 2-3 grams per liter of water
Application: Spray every 10-15 days, 2-3 times

Safety: Wear protective gear. Do not spray during flowering. Follow 7-day pre-harvest interval.

Organic Alternatives

Neem oil spray (5ml per liter)
Baking soda solution (1 tsp per liter)
Garlic-chili extract spray

Prevention Tips

  • Ensure proper plant spacing for air circulation
  • Avoid overhead watering, water at soil level
  • Remove infected plant debris regularly
  • Use disease-resistant varieties when available

Seasonal Pest Patterns

  • • Thrips activity increases

  • • Bacterial infections rise
  • • Prepare for summer pests
